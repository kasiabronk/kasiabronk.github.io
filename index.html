UCL Doctoral Skills Development Programme


Welcome to UCL Doctoral Skills Development Programme

The Doctoral Skills Development Programme is open to all postgraduate researchers at UCL. The purpose of the programme is to give you the opportunity to expand your research and transferable skills in order to support your research, professional development and employability. Transferable skills training for researchers is an important part of UCL 2034 strategy, Doctoral Strategy and the QAA Quality Code for Higher Education.

On this website, you can find details of the programme of practical skills training courses and workshops running this academic year, as well as general information and useful links in relation transferable skills training. Here you are able to register for training courses and manage your skills training portfolio through the on-line system
